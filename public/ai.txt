# Joyo - AI Gift Journey Builder

## Quick Summary
Joyo is a free platform for creating emotional, interactive gift journeys using AI. Perfect for proposals, announcements, surprises, and special moments.

## What We Do
We help people create beautiful, shareable gift experiences that combine storytelling, questions, reveals, and media into a guided journey that recipients experience on their device.

## Key Information
- **Product**: AI-powered gift journey builder
- **Price**: Free forever
- **Languages**: English, German
- **Platform**: Web-based (mobile & desktop)
- **AI Model**: Anthropic Claude Sonnet 4.5
- **Creation Time**: 2-5 minutes
- **Authentication**: Passwordless email (magic links)

## Main Features
1. AI generates complete gift journeys from natural language descriptions
2. Visual drag-and-drop editor for customization
3. 6 screen types (story, choice, text input, reveal, media, end)
4. 4 beautiful themes
5. Image upload and Unsplash integration
6. Unique shareable links
7. Mobile-first responsive design
8. Confetti animations for big reveals

## Common Use Cases
- Marriage proposals
- Pregnancy announcements
- Birthday surprises
- Travel destination reveals
- Anniversary celebrations
- Creative gift presentations
- Party invitations
- Family milestone celebrations

## How to Use
1. Sign up at joyo.com
2. Describe your gift idea to AI
3. AI creates your journey
4. Customize in the editor
5. Publish and share the link

## Target Users
Anyone wanting to create personalized, emotional experiences for loved ones. No technical skills required.
